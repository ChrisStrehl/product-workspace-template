# discovery/ — How We Learn

Where understanding gets built before it becomes work: research questions, interviews, and opportunity solution trees (goals → opportunities → solutions).

## Suggested structure
- `ost/` — opportunity solution trees
- `campaigns/` — research cycles, with interviews inside
- `research-questions.md` — prioritized question backlog
- `segments.md` — who you talk to + how to recruit them

## Adapt for your role
Discovery isn't only user interviews — it's whatever reduces your biggest uncertainty:
- **PM:** user & stakeholder interviews, experiments
- **Engineer:** technical spikes, prototypes, benchmarks
- **UX:** usability testing, research synthesis
- **Data:** exploratory analysis, hypothesis testing

**Flow:** `BACKLOG.md` → research questions → campaigns/interviews → opportunities → solutions → an initiative.
